## Historical Context: How We Got Here

The increased scrutiny of cryptocurrencies and the implementation of filtering systems by major platforms like X are the culmination of years of unregulated growth in the crypto space. Emerging technologies introduced decentralized finances, resulting in superior investment opportunities. However, they simultaneously opened the floodgates to fraudsters targeting unsuspecting users. The advent of countless Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) and the anonymity provided by blockchain technology made it easier for scammers to exploit potential investors. Regulatory bodies worldwide have struggled to keep pace with this rapid evolution.

In Ukraine, the government recognized the need for regulation as digital assets gained traction.
